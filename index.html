The small but great country Malta

Malta Beaches

Golden Bay

In the northwestern part of the island of Malta you will find some of the best beaches in Malta. The most famous and crowded is Golden Bay. Basically because one of the most popular resorts on the island is located on its shores, the Radisson Blu Golden Sands. There are also several restaurants, a large parking lot and the bus stop next door. Access is super comfortable and easy!

Sweethaven Village

Popeye Village, also known as Sweethaven Village, is a group of rustic wooden buildings located in Anchor Bay, northwest of the Mediterranean island of Malta, two miles from the town of Melliehun.

Traditional Maltese cuisine

Pastizzi

This little puff pastry with ricotta or pea paste is undoubtedly one of the most popular snacks on the island. You can find it in any bakery or bar-restaurant and always at a very good price. The Maltese eat it very often for breakfast or a snack and in general a unit of these pastries can cost between 0.30 and 0.50 euro cents.

Bigilla

This is the version that exists in Malta of hummus. It consists of a purple bean pate that is combined with crushed garlic, parsley and chili pepper and olive oil is added to everything. It is usually served as an appetizer accompanied with bread and goat cheese. Be sure to try it since it is a delight of Maltese gastronomy.
